About Me

I am a Research Scholar at the Department of Chemistry, Indian Institute of Technology, Gandhinagar, specializing in computational molecular design for highly efficient organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs).

Research Interests

  • Organic semiconductors, excited state dynamics, and optoelectronics
  • Molecular Dynamics and Kinetic Monte Carlo simulations for charge transport properties
  • Machine learning techniques to explore molecular chemical space
  • Materials for blue organic light-emitting diodes, Catalysis

Current Focus

My research focuses on developing computational workflows to design highly efficient OLED materials through multiscale modeling approaches, combining quantum chemical calculations with machine learning techniques to accelerate the discovery of next-generation optoelectronic materials.
